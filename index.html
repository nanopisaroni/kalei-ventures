About

Kalei Ventures

Kalei stands for Kaleidoscope — a symbol of the diversity of thought, background, ethnicity and gender that defines our team. We are a venture capital fund investing in Latin American startups that are committed to applying innovative solutions in emerging markets, with a clear focus on international expansion and category leadership.

With 18 portfolio companies across Argentina, Mexico, Colombia, Chile, Brazil, Uruguay, and the United States, we perform deal sourcing across the region and back founders at the intersection of technical capability and commercial viability.

Investment Thesis

What we look for

We invest at the intersection of technical capability and commercial viability — where AI meets actual customer needs in Latin America's emerging markets.

01

Real clients, not demos

We look for startups that already have paying customers or signed pilots. No science projects — we fund companies whose AI solves a problem someone is already paying to fix.

02

Measurable impact

If the AI can't show a before-and-after in a concrete business metric — cost reduction, revenue lift, time saved — it's not ready. We back what can be measured.

03

Founder-market fit

We invest in founders who deeply understand the problem domain — not just the technology. Domain expertise beats generic technical brilliance every time.

04

Capital efficiency

Latin American startups need to do more with less. We fund teams that can reach meaningful milestones without burning through rounds of venture capital.
